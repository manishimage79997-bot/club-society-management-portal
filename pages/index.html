About the Portal

One Platform for Every
Club & Society

The Don Bosco University Clubs & Societies Portal is a centralised digital platform designed to streamline how student organisations operate, communicate, and grow.

🏛️

Club Registration

Register new clubs and societies, manage memberships, and maintain official rosters — all in one place.

📅

Event Management

Plan, announce, and manage campus events with approvals, schedules, and participant tracking built in.

📢

Announcements

Send notices, updates, and circulars directly to club members and relevant stakeholders instantly.

🗂️

Digital Records

Maintain digital records of activities, achievements, and member contributions for every chapter.
